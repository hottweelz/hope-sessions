# About Paul Andrejack

Paul Andrejack is a recording artist, producer, audio engineer, and studio owner based in Denver, North Carolina. He is the founder and driving force behind Hope Sessions, a recording initiative operating out of PMA Recording Studios in Denver, NC.

## Career Background

Paul built his career on Long Island, New York over more than 20 years — constructing his own recording studio, fronting and collaborating with multiple bands, recording and releasing albums, and completing multiple European tours as a working recording and performing artist. That combination of studio ownership, band leadership, and live touring is what he brings into everything he does in Denver, NC today.

## Recording and Production Credits

Paul Andrejack is an Internet Records recording artist, songwriter, and producer. His professional history includes:

- **Co-written with Grammy Award winners**: Mark Mangold (Cher, Jordin Sparks) and Mark Gatz (The Cosby Show)
- **Collaborated with The Alessi Brothers**: known for their work alongside Paul McCartney and John Lennon
- **Music placement**: Food Network's Bobby Flay show
- **Studio consulting**: Bakithi Kumalo (bassist for Paul Simon, Josh Groban, Gloria Estefan) and Kent Marcum (composer for Harpo Productions)
- **Director of A&R**: oversaw consultants, scouted talent, and guided artists through music marketing and product development
- **Artist manager**: Extreme Management Group

## Studio and Live Sound

Paul operated PRO AUDIO SOUND — a PA rental company he ran for five years, providing sound for festivals and venues including the LI Music Festival. His studio in Denver, NC runs analog and digital consoles (Tascam DM-3200, Mackie, Peavey, Soundcraft) with outboard gear from Focusrite, Avalon, Lexicon, TC Electronic, Slate, Waves, Pultec, and Fairchild.
