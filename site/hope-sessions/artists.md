# For Artists — Hope Sessions

## Who is Hope Sessions for?

Hope Sessions is built for artists across Lincoln County and the greater Denver, NC area who need a real path to professional recording and experienced guidance that makes work better. That includes:

- Songwriters and performers at any experience level
- Musicians who have original material but have never had access to a professional studio
- Artists with strong ideas but no recording history or professional credits
- Beginners who have never been inside a recording studio

The standard for participation is the strength of the idea, not the length of an artist's credits.

## Do I need prior recording experience to apply?

No. Hope Sessions explicitly does not require prior studio experience, a recording history, or professional credits. If you have original ideas — songs, concepts, or material you've been developing — and you are based in the Lincoln County or greater Denver, NC area, you are encouraged to reach out and start a conversation.

## What does participating in Hope Sessions involve?

Participating artists work at PMA Recording Studios in Denver, NC with Paul Andrejack, who brings 20+ years of engineering, production, and artist development experience. Sessions are tailored to where you are as an artist:

- For first-timers: understanding the signal chain, building scratch tracks, and finding what the song actually needs before committing to a full session
- For developing artists: production guidance, arrangement feedback, and tracking with professional equipment
- For experienced performers: applying professional engineering and production judgment to work you know is strong, and finishing it to a releasable standard

Every artist leaves with a completed recording — not a rough draft, but work that can exist in the world.
