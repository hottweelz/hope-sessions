Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Applies to: hopesessions.pmarecordingstudios.com

What Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your organization or role (optional)
  • Your message
  • The inquiry type you selected

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, license, or transfer your information to any third party.

Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use analytics software (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels, no ad retargeting cookies).

This site does load fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google may log your IP address per their own privacy policy. We have no control over or access to that data. You can review Google’s Privacy Policy (opens in a new tab) for details.

When you dismiss the privacy notice on this site, a flag is stored in your browser’s localStorage (not a cookie — it is not transmitted to any server and is only used to avoid showing the notice again).

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to Spotify, LinkedIn, and other external sites. Clicking those links will take you to third-party services with their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are a resident of the European Union or California, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you. Because the only personal data we hold is what you submitted through our contact form, requests are simple to fulfill.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at pmastudios@hotmail.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.” We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to and follow up on your inquiry. We do not maintain a marketing database or subscriber list from form submissions.
