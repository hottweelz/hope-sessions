# ASC Verification Note

## Verified From Public Sources On April 13, 2026

### Gateway And Geography

- Lincoln County is included in the Artist Support Grants consortium administered by Arts & Science Council Charlotte-Mecklenburg.
- The North Carolina Arts Council statewide Artist Support page lists Arts & Science Council Charlotte-Mecklenburg as the local administrator for Cabarrus, Cleveland, Gaston, Lincoln, Mecklenburg, and Rowan Counties.

### Public Deadline And Award Information

- The statewide NC Arts Council Artist Support page lists the Lincoln County consortium deadline as September 7, 2026.
- The statewide NC Arts Council page lists the Lincoln County consortium grant range as $500 to $3,000.
- The ASC local page confirms that artists may request up to $3,000.
