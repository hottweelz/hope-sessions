## Why This Team

- WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility discipline
- stronger public narrative and partner trust

## Quality Gates

1. The page must support the grant package rather than distract from it.
2. No launch without real proof elements.
3. WCAG 2.2 AA compliance requires manual testing, not only automated scans.
4. The page must remain lightweight and fast on mobile.
5. The studio must be framed as infrastructure, not the funded mission.
