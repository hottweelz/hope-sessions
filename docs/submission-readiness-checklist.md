# Submission Readiness Checklist

An application is not ready until every item below is answered.

## Eligibility

- Correct geography confirmed
- Correct entity type confirmed
- Residency requirement confirmed
- Fiscal sponsor requirement confirmed

## Narrative

- Project is framed around outcomes, not business subsidy
- Community impact is specific
- Budget narrative matches project narrative
- Work samples support the ask

## Attachments

- Bio included
- Work samples linked
- Budget attached
- Timeline attached
- Letters of support attached if required

## Risk Review

- Reality Checker review complete
- Legal Compliance Checker review complete
- Finance review complete
- Final owner approved submission
