# Narrative Bank Template

## One-Sentence Positioning

Write the shortest accurate version of the mission.

## Mission Statement

Describe what the studio enables for artists and community.

## Need Statement

Describe the local problem the project addresses.

## Project Description

Describe the exact project being funded.

## Community Impact

Describe who benefits, how they benefit, and what is measurable.

## Artistic Merit

Describe why the work matters creatively.

## Access Statement

Describe how the studio expands access to professional recording or learning.

## Cultural Preservation Angle

Describe any regional or genre documentation value.

## Equipment Justification

Describe equipment only in terms of output, access, quality, or delivery.

## Partnership Statement

Describe how outside organizations, schools, churches, or community groups fit in.

## Boilerplate Bio

Write a short applicant bio and a longer applicant bio.
